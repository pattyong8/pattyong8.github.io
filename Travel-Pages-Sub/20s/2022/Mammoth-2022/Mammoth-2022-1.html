Mammoth Climbing 2022

  • With Noah, Iris, AJ
  • Jul 13th-16th 2022
  • Mammoth, CA

I finally attended my first large-scale music festival! Since I wasn't sure if I wanted to commit to all three days of Outside Lands, I only bought a one-day pass for Saturday. In retrospect, it was the right call, and I'm glad I went. I got to see Green Day, Jack Harlow, and Polo & Pan. Other fun memories include eating ube desserts, drinking tons of craft beverages, and spending quality time with my friends. It was an unforgettable experience and a fantastic introduction to the festival scene.
