Yosemite EC's Bday 2022

  • With ES Besties & Co.
  • Jun 25th-27th 2022
  • Yosemite, CA

Esther decided to celebrate her birthday in Yosemite and invited friends from various backgrounds to an Airbnb near the park entrance. Throughout the weekend, we bonded over hiking, cooking delicious meals, and enjoying each other's company. Esther, especially, treated us to a fantastic Asian-inspired birthday feast that was absolutely delicious!

Day 1

We all arrived at the Airbnb Esther had booked late on Friday night after driving up together. Despite the late hour, we quickly settled in, picked our beds, and prepared for sleep. However, before calling it a night, we made sure to celebrate Esther's birthday by bringing out a cake and wishing her a fantastic weekend ahead. It was wonderful meeting all of Esther's friends from different circles, and it was clear why she surrounded herself with such genuine and caring individuals. We were all eagerly anticipating Day 2: the Mist Trail hike.

Day 2

The next day, we rose early to ensure we could enter the park and secure a good parking spot. Despite our early start, finding parking proved challenging upon our arrival. Fortunately, luck was on our side, and we managed to snag a few remaining spots in the Mist Trail parking lot. After parking, we gathered our gear, applied sunscreen, filled our camelbacks, and took a quick bathroom break to prepare for our hike. Esther had prepared a delightful chicken salad mixture to enjoy between slices of sourdough bread for lunch, which had everyone looking forward to our midday meal already!

We started our hike on the Mist Trail with an easy, flat path that had minimal incline. However, a few miles in, the trail transformed into a steady upward climb. Thankfully, our group was in good shape and continued up the trail with determination. Everyone was crushing it! On the ascent, we didn't talk much since we were all huffing and puffing from the intense cardio workout. Despite the physical challenge, the sights were breathtaking. The trail was bustling with fellow hikers, as it was a popular time of year to visit. We made fun memories along the way, like taking photos of everyone on the misty sections of the trail, conquering the main part of the steps, and even passing many other hikers. The shared effort and beautiful scenery made the experience unforgettable.

We finally arrived at the famous part of the Mist Trail, and it was a truly wonderful sight. Even though it was summer and the mist wasn't at full capacity, we still got sprayed, which felt amazing on such a hot day with minimal shade. We had just a few more miles to go before reaching the top of the Mist Trail, where the trail continued onto the Half Dome portion. Once we reached the top, we took a well-deserved rest for about an hour. We enjoyed the delicious chicken salad sandwiches Esther had prepared and listened to some music, soaking in the beauty of our surroundings. After such an intense 13-mile vertical hike, the break was a much-needed and rewarding respite.

On the way down, we were able to talk a lot more. I was hiking near Joan, and we engaged in a deep conversation about relationships and our life aspirations. We shared stories about past significant others, current people we were seeing, and our overall desires in a life partner. This conversation brought Joan and me closer, strengthening our friendship. Fun memories from the descent included climbing a 15-foot wall (rated V0) near the bottom of the hike, dipping our entire bodies into the freezing river to aid muscle recovery, and getting a close-up view of Half Dome. It was an exhilarating and memorable day!

The day was packed with activities and did not disappoint. Once we got back to the Airbnb, we were all exhausted but somehow found the energy to prepare a full feast, led by the incredible Esther herself. I honestly have no idea how she managed to whip up a meal that felt Michelin star-worthy after such an intense day. We sat down together and enjoyed the meal outdoors, savoring every bite. I was stuffed afterward! To end the night, we relaxed in the hot tub and chilled for the rest of the evening. It was a fantastic birthday weekend where I got closer to some of my greatest friends, creating unforgettable memories.
