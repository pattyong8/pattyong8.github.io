Family in SF 2022

  • With Family
  • Jul 1st-24th 2022
  • San Francisco, CA

Having my family visit during the summer in San Francisco was such a treat. It was amazing to explore the city I live in with two of the most important people in my life. Vinny stayed in the city for the entire month of July, subletting from a friend, which allowed us to spend some long-overdue quality time together. Later in the month, Mom also came to visit. We dined at some of my favorite restaurants in SF, explored the tourist attractions, and enjoyed a few drinks. The memories that stand out include dinner at San Ho Wan, rock climbing with Vinny, and partying with Vinny and Erin!
