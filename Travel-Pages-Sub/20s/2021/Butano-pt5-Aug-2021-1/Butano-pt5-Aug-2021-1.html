Butano, CA Pt.5

  • With Friends
  • Aug 13th-Aug 15th 2021
  • Weekend Getaway

Butano the gem. The perfect weekend getaway to recharge and reset. We had a great group with a few newcomers! I slept in the bunkhouse for the first time, almost didn’t do the cliff jump, and didn’t cook a meal once while I was there. These are some of my greatest friends in life and I was glad that I could spend quality time with them before starting my new gig at Eastdil Secured.

Pictures

Fun memories include…

I am obviously the more jumpy individual in our friend group. My friends decided to try and scare me while I was in the bunkhouse. They failed because I happened to leave the bunkhouse right as they were sneaking up. The rest of the time we were there, they were jokingly talking about a witch being in the woods in an attempt to frighten me.

I usually help with cooking and love cooking for large groups of people, but this time, I did not have to do any cooking. I didn’t even think about it!

I had done the cliff jump before but this time I randomly psyched myself out and had a hard time jumping off. It took me a good 5mins. A random Uranine man was taking videos of us and I showed him how to airdrop them to my phone lol.

I slept in the bunkhouse for the first time ever! It was Nate, myself, and Cohen. On the last night, we had a deepish conversation about my dating life and tendency not to put myself out there. They were both extremely supportive!

I thought I could Uber/Lyft from the Pescadero beach front but this was not true. There were very limited cars available in this area. Gus had to drive me to Half-Moon Bay and I was able to get a ride there. I almost didn’t think I could match there as well so I contacted Dino and his dad almost ended up giving me a ride.

Kiki and I became amazing friends overnight. The other newcomers were Cohen and Augu.
