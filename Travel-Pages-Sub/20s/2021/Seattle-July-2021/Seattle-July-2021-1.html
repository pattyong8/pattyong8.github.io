Seattle July 2021

  • With Amazon Crew
  • July 16th-19th 2021
  • Visiting friends in Seattle

It had been around a 1½ since I last saw all my friends up in Seattle. Eric, Jot, Mihir, and I made plans to visit everyone in Seattle in July. It was so nice to see everyone again and reminisce on the old days. I continue to hold all of these people close in my life even though I do not see them often. Eric and I were able to crash at Danny’s place and hangout together. I got some bouldering in, hung out by a lake, and hit up Rhino room lol.

Pictures

When I landed, I met up with Eric at a restaurant at the Airport where we got beers and food provided by the Chase priority pass benefit. I was taking full advantage of it and it was well worth it. We had a heart to heart about current life on the train to Zennie’s. I am glad I got this time with Eric. We then saw Zennie’s new place and then drove to her old place to hangout on her roof. Danny eventually met us there and we ended up staying until very late before heading out to the social scene in Cap Hill. Sometime in between, we hit up Optimism. Danny and I won a BP game on Zennie’s roof against Noah and Alec. We ended up hitting Rhino room that night which was a blast. Mihir didn’t get into the club because he talked to the bouncer and apparently was too drunk lol.

We stayed out pretty late. At one point, Danny and I left Eric at Rhino room since he was talking to a girl. We tried getting pizza but the place was closed. The next morning, we did brunch with Danny’s girlfriend Sabriana. We originally tried to go out for brunch but all the restaurants were packed. :( We ended up grabbing groceries and making a fantastic brunch ourselves. We then went to SBP to boulder. Danny and Eric were pretty popped after 30 mins so I told them to head out since Zennie was on her way with Marisa. We bouldered for a good amount of time before heading to a Vietnamese restaurant. Zennie paid which was very nice of her! I love the natural back and forth of our friendship.

That night we went to another club and partied the night away. We waited in line for around 45 mins. While in line, I stuck up a conversation with someone in front of me that was also involved in the world of real estate. He gave me some intel for Virginia real estate lol. The next morning, we got brunch with all our friends at one of the houses and then headed to Lake Merritt to hangout for the afternoon. I kicked our friends across the lake to a platform with a slide since they were not great swimmers. Thankfully, they have inflatable donuts. Tried doing a front flip but ended up back flopping. We sat around in the sun. Jack invited up back to his place for dinner to eat some wild caught Alaskan salmon he caught a few weeks back. We happily accepted his offer.

We went to a super bougie market to grab some supplies. It was so expensive lol. Jack’s place was actually amazing. Lots of space and a nice rustic vibe. I made some dank mashed potatoes. I got Iced by Jack lol. We had many conversation topics but one memorable one in particular was a conversation around how politics gets brought up during family occasions. I personally had never experienced it since I do not have extended family in the US. It was funny to see how easy it is to tie anything back to politics. After this we headed back and retired for the night. What an amazing trip with some amazing friends.
