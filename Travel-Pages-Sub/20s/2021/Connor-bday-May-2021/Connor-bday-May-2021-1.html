Connor's 24th Birthday 2021

  • With Scott Street & SF Friends
  • May 25th 2021
  • Sushi Delight & House Party

Now that everyone was vaccinated or had COVID, Connor was itching to celebrate his birthday in a big way. We went to sushi delight for all you can eat sushi + all you can drink sake bombs. The night eventually ended up at our place for some drinking games and dancing. I got to see a few people I hadn’t seen in awhile and celebrate one of my best friend’s birthdays.
