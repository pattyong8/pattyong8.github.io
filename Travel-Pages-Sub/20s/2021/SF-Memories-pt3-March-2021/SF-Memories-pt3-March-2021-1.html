SF Memories Pt.3

  • With Scott Street & SF friends
  • March-May 2021
  • Back in the Bay

The COVID 19 Pandemic was coming to a close in California, I was able to get both of my vaccine shots! I got the Pfizer shots. I got a gym membership at this point and was lifting at my neighborhood gym as well as climbing at Dogpatch. Jack got into climbing for a bit! I went on a few dates during this time. For Sarah’s birthday we went houseboating near Marin. Accenture sponsored the Golden State Warriors for LGBTQ+ night so I was able to go since I was on the Accenture NorCal office LGBTQ+ leadership team. That was the first basketball in suite experience I have ever had! Mom also came and visited before taking off for Taiwan.

The new gym I was lifting at is called SF fitness. It was a pretty nice gym and cost around $100 a month. I got full use out of the gym since I was going around 5 days a week. I got Jack into climbing! Because of this, we all ended up getting year long memberships. Rishi sold me his Lulu bag which I wasn’t going to buy at first but then decided it would be a good investment. Proved to be correct. It was around this time that I decided to look for new roles outside of Accenture. I remember I had a connection with someone from home in the Real Estate world. I asked them to be connected to one of the most prominent Commercial Real Estate firms in the country and the rest is history. I had to study my ass off though...

It was during this time that crypto trading was going berserk. All types of crypto were flying +10% each day. It was good that Davis and I were able to make use of that situation. Went on a hike with Ryan, Sarah, and Ungar in SF. Because of the new gym, Rishi got me and Connor swim caps! Wooohooo! My wrists started to hurt a bit so I had to get a second wrist brace. Hopefully, this does not become too big of a thing.
