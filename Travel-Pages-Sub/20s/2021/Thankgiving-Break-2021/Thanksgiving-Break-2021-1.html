Thanksgiving Break

  • With Family
  • Nov 23rd-Nov 28th 2021
  • Irvine, CA

When I started at Eastdil, I thought it would be hard to find time to go home and that my holidays would be full of work. Thankfully, the pandemic altered the way even a real estate company viewed working from the office which allowed me to work remotely for most of the holiday season. This started with Thanksgiving break. We had a traditional Thanksgiving dinner with Doug’s family and I got to catch up with Davis and JR.

Pictures

This was a very relaxing break with a few activities. Mom really wanted to get photos at the Fashion Island Christmas tree so we spent the first day back having a mini photoshoot. I saw Davis the next day and we caught up over Bear Flag poke. Thanksgiving was next. We went to Doug’s parents house and had a traditional Thanksgiving dinner. Honestly, everyone hates Turkey but I think my mom makes an amazing full turkey. It was extremely juicy and went well with the stuffing Doug made. We were surprised by bottles full of Martinelli's Sparkling Apple Cider. I probably drank a whole bottle to myself. I saw Davis again after Thanksgiving and we gave Taiko another shot. Honestly, it was not as great as it used to be but it was still sushi. I also got to see JR! It was really nice catching up.
