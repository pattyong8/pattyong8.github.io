Harry Potter and the Cursed Child

  • With Reena Sundan
  • Jan 30th & 31st, 2020
  • Curran Theater

One of my good friends from my Accenture start class, Reena Sudan, asked if I want to accompany her to the play “Harry Potter and the Cursed Child”. I am 100% a Harry Potter fan so decided to join her! What I didn’t know was that this play was split into two 3-4 hour segments. Also, it was the most expensive play, ever lol. It didn’t matter though because it was AMAZING! The special effects were pehnomical, acting superb, and screenwriting stupendous.
