Friendsgiving 2020

  • With SF Friends
  • November 20th, 2020
  • Scott Street, SF

We had a wonderful Friendsgiving with our COVID bubble group. Although there wasn’t the traditional turkey, the traditional friends and warm vibes were present. This was a refreshing break from not seeing many people for many months on end. COVID was a black swan event that led to a number of friendships fading away and a handful that became stronger than I could ever imagine. Cheers to my chosen family.

This Thanksgiving meal was much needed since COVID was such an isolating period of time. Being together with friends was so important. Thankfully, more than half of the group was vaccinated and most of the rest already had it. It was a safe environment to enjoy a dinner with our COVID group. I made mashed potatoes and gravy. Others brought or bought sides. Hannah brought a rosiserty chicken from Suvla. Olivia didn’t disappoint and brought 5 plates of dessert!!!
