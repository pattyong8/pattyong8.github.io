Back In Berkeley 2019

  • With CAL Friends
  • August 2019
  • Music Festival & SF Park

After traveling parts of Asia and being home for the summer, I returned to Berkeley to live in the beautiful house known as Virgo, start my career at Accenture, and continue my college friendships. This week was thematic due to the many reunions that occurred. There was a day in the park hanging out with some of my best friends in college, Jake’s 21st birthday party, and the Big Game.

The first day I got back, I went to hangout at Akbar and Ahannah’s place in Berkeley. We had a ton of friends over, had a potluck, played games, and caught up with each other. Akbar and Ahannah made tandori style chicken, curry, and a medley of other items. Funny story, after we were all ready to leave, I left and got dropped off at the Bart station to bart into the city for my training with Accenture. Apparently, Bart was not running on a Sunday night at Shattuck so I had to Uber into the city. Turns out the Uber that picked me up was the same one that Daniel Stephens took. We ended up in the same Uber pool! What are the chances haha.

The following weekend, our entire group went to a music festival in the city and enjoyed a pop up vendor shop. It was beautiful and amazing. The live music was not my style but still quite enjoyable. We eventually ended up in the park and spent many hours basking in the sun and drinking beers. Couple of other interesting events include... Jake’s 21st bay celebration was in the city at his sister's place. We ended up at a Irish bar called Shannon’s. Because Jake was 21, 21 different people had to give him a kiss on the cheek. So much fun! Also, the Big Game happened and I saw Vincent during it! Was such a good time.
