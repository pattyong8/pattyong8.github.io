My first client was such a unique and amazing first project experience. Many folks come into Accenture with grandiose expectations for travel + dinner but soon after joining realize that Accenture doesn’t really do that. Turns out my first project ever was an exception and way above expectations. For my client project, we had to work through the holidays but my boss was super understanding and gave our team time off before and after the project. I worked remotely for the first 2 weeks before the 28th of December, came back in time for cutover, worked through New Years, and went back home for 2 weeks after. So nice! When going back home the first time, Justin was willing to drive me to the San Jose megabus stop. Because of this, we decided to climb, grab dinner, meet up with Kate, and go to a random bar to drink haha. They are such amazing friends lol. When I was in the bay area for New Years, Connor and I went to go celebrate with Sahar and Lucas in the city. We had wine, went to a nearby wine bar, and talked all night. The next morning, we went to get little sheep.
