Starting Adult Life 2019

  • With Accenture Friends & Colleagues
  • Sept-March 2019
  • Orientation Viavi, & TriNet

My adult life has officially started! After graduating college, I accepted a full-time role with Accenture as a Management Consultant. I meet some amazing friends in my start class, colleges through my projects, and acquaintances through office interactions. Thankfully, my start date and graduation prevented me from starting my first role remotely. Because of this, I was able to have the best of both worlds; some time was spent traveling as a consultant would and some time was spent in a work from home setup. Honestly, this was probably one of the best ways I could have started my adult life.

My start class was a very fun group. We had lots of happy hours, met up with each other on the weekends, and even had a killer friendsgiving. Some of my favorite memories include the following… I became great friends with Justin Fell, one of my fellow co-workers. We grabbed coffee and breakfast at Starbucks on most mornings before work and booled around a bit before everyone else showed up. Justin soon became known for this chocolate chip cookie and milk breakfast order. We both made fun of him for that.

I tried to convince many folks in my start class to go climbing with me. Absolutely no surprise there lol. I remember when I first started at the office, I was amazed by the view from our office. I mean, we worked at the FREAKING SALESFORCE TOWER. So cool haha. Accenture actually had so many happy hours and group events. It provided for a perfect opportunity to get to know others and network every friday afternoon. Sometime towards the end of my training period, Nate and Matt came and visited for a game day. It was so fun to have them around!

Other fun memories

1. My co-workers thought Connor was attractive and there was a stint where I tried to set him up with a few of them. Not officially though haha.

2. I was getting stronger and stronger at climbing. V5-V6 was not attainable. Progress!

3. Friday’s turned out to be a day to look forward to during the week. This was not only because it was the end of the work week but because it was also an opportunity to hangout with my work friends and attend some real cool events at bars and restaurants I would never go to on my own.

Through the PRIDE ERG, I won a seat in a raffle at Accenture’s courtside box. The event was a John Mayer concert! To start off the after work festivities, Justin and I decided to climb before the concert. We went to Dogpatch and then got drinks at a nearby bar. After climbing, Justin accompanied me to the Chase center where we met up with Lindsey who also got a seat! Unfortunately, Justin did not have a seat so we said our goodbyes.

The concert was amazing and the amenities were even better! Holy, this courtside box was so close to the concert itself! I could clearly see John Mayer and the rest of the band. Fun memories… One of the Managing directors that went bought a couple of us a round of drinks. The actual suite itself was amazing. Very fancy and there were massive television sets all around the room.

I wasn’t a huge John Mayer fan going into the concert but really enjoyed my time and became a fan afterwards!
