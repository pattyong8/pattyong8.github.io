Irvine March/April 2020

  • With Family
  • Mar 17th-Apr 13th 2020
  • Home during beginning of COVID-19

When COVID hit, I decided to go back home for a bit. Vincent and I made the road trip down from Berkeley. Being home for this long was something I thought I wouldn’t be able to do after starting my full-time job. I cherished this opportunity to work from home, hangout with family, and spend time at home. We even got to celebrate my birthday during COVID-19! Highlights include all the wonderful home meals, Vinebox with my Accenture team, and a multitude of video chat catch up calls with my friends.

I planned a fun wine tasting event for my Accenture team at TriNet. We used a company called Vinebox to provide wines for the tasting [Pictures #8 & #9]. I was actually very happy with the product and how the event turned out.

Mom wanted to go all out for this happy hour, so she bought a variety of different cheeses, crackers, and even the fancy meats that you usually are provided at wine tastings.

My entire team brought our +1s and started the wine tasting. Honestly, it wasn’t until this point that I was able to taste the difference between so many different types of wines. After the event, I had the idea of sharing a Vinebox with my friends at Virgo for a spontaneous wine tasting night.

Because of COVID-19, my friends and I had to find creative ways to catch up with each other [Pictures #26, #27, #28 & #29]. There was a lot of Zoom calls because Zoom at this time offered free 45min session accounts to all users. Tons of people hopped on this platform to host group calls since it was one of the best for a gallery view and virtual displays.

I got to chat with my friends at Virgo while home in SoCal. Half of us were home and half of us were in other places. I also facetimed with Ryan and Quinn to play league haha. I was getting back into the game and was trying to get all my friends to play with me. I also had a ton of calls with Reena and Catherine. These were mostly for the NA PITCH competition we decided to compete in. Most of these calls were either in the early morning or late evening to accommodate for different time zones. Lastly, I was able to chat up with the travel squad I was a part of in China. This included Antonia, Jake, Ryan, and Connor. We were able to talk about the memories we had and potential plans to meet up in the near future.
