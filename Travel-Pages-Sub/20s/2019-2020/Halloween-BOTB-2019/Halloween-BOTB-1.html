Halloween & BOTB 2019

  • With Virgo + Climbing homies
  • Oct 31st-Nov 1st, 2019
  • Halloween in SF and Battle of the Bay climbing competition at Dogpatch

For Halloween, I celebrated this year with many different groups (my Accenture team, my Accenture start class, and my Berkeley friends). The Virgo house decided to go out to SF to celebrate. There was this bar hopping event that we pre-paid for. Each of the bars checked for a wristband that we got and gave us discounted drinks. So much fun! We even bumped into many Berkeley students a year below us! After Halloween, Rishi, Trevor, Olivia, Robb, Josh, and I attended the Dogpatch Battle of the Bay climbing competition. We both were able to climb in the public round and watch the finals for the pro climbers! What an event!

Each of the Halloween celebrations were amazing. For Accenture, my boss wanted me to get Halloween costumes so that we could participate in our client’s Halloween parade. For the climbing competition, I signed myself up for the intermediate bracket since the advanced bracket was all V6s and above. I wasn’t confident that I would be able to do that many V6s haha. I ended up scoring a good number of points in my own bracket! Woooohooo! Olivia and Rishi finished their climbing a bit early so they ended up taking seats at the bleachers and got amazing freaking seats! OMG I loved them for that because we were able to see the climbs up close and personal. What a treat! :) Our entry covered beer and pizza which was nice haha.
