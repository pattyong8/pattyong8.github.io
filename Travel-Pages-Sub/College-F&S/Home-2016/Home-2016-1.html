Summer Break 2016

This was my first summer back from college and represented my slow transition into the real world. I had an internship with a hedge fund, got to hangout with my family, and rekindled some CDM friendships. This was a milestone summer because it was now just me, my mom, brother in the new Univeristy Hill complex. Being able to do whatever we wanted to do and seeing mom happy was breathtaking. My internship at Rimrock Captial opened my eyes to the work of finance and lucrative nature of this line of work. Outside of work, most of my time was spent hanging out with the GC, some Y&G friends, and the HVST family.

KBBQ Grill Night

On one of these mid-summer nights, my family and I ventured to the new commnuity grill in hopes of cooking up a juicy KBBQ dinner. Vincent, our designated grillmaster, fired up the grill while my Mom and I took some artsy pictures. While the meal was cooking, we took some family photos and enjoyed the sunset. Moments like these would be hard to come by after both Vincent and took off for college so we always found a way to cherish them with a photoshoot. ;)

Rimrock Capital Internship

Rimrock Capital was a rare opportunity to experiene the surprisingly posh lifestyle of finance. Everyday, we got food catered from $$-$$$ resturants and cafes, had access to any type of beverage, and had our own multi-monitor desk. I have to say that I did not expect this to be my first college internship but sure glad I landed the role. It exposed me to just the right amount of the finance work to spark my interest.

CDM Friends

While at home, I hungout with a good handful of my high school friends. There was of course the GC, the Y&G squad, and a few others that I got to see. This was the last year that we did a formal and official NFL fantasy draft. I was still very much into HOTS and played often with Vincent and Andres. Once again, this summer served as a great transition period from high school to college.
