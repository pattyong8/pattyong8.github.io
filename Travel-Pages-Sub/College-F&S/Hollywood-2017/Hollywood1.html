Hollywood 2017

  • With Mom and Vincent
  • May 14th, 2017
  • Hike and Day Trip

We have been to Hollywood a number of times but never did one of the Hollywood sign hikes. Over the summer of 2017, we decided it was about time. Our goal was to get as close to the sign as we possibly could. When deciding on which hike to take, we chose the hardest but shortest one. After the Hollywood sign, we went to the Griffith Observatory and walked around the Hollywood shopping district.
