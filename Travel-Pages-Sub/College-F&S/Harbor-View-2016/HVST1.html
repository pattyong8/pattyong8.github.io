Harbor View Swim Team 2016

  • With Todd, Tom, Steph, Ben, Maye, and the HVST Family
  • Summer 2016
  • Neighborhood Swim Team

This was my second season as a swim coach with the Harbor View Swim Team and the second time that we won at league finals! I did a combination of sports camp, swim coaching, and swim lessons. I was not able to swim this season since I was 19 years old and the oldest age group was 15-18 years of age. This year, I was able to work closely with a couple of swimmers: Dalton, Hudson, Brody, MeiMei, Oliver, and Stone. Being a swim coach for HVST is more than just a job. It was an environment for me to practice my leadership skills and teaching abilities.

Lip Sync

HVST has a very fun tradition of hosting a lip sync skits night where all swimmers can pair up with groups to put on a show. All the coaches usually come together and create their own skit. This year, we did a rendition of a synchronized swimming performance. It was poorly executed but very entertaining for the kids. The 15-18 year old age group which included my brother, Davis, Connor, Garrett, Kevin, Robert, and Maye did a Mulan bottle flip skit. So funny.

League Finals

League Finals was at CDM this year and we won the entire thing! The hard work that all our swimmers put in throughout the summer really paid off. At the end of the meet, our entire team jumped into the pool and took a group picture.
