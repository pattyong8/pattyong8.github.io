San Diego 2017

  • With Mom and Vincent
  • March 30th, 2017
  • Fun Day Trip

What a fun and spontaneous trip to San Diego! Mom decided randomly that she wanted to go visit the flower fields of Carlsbad, so we made a day trip out of it. First, we started with a hike to potato chip rock. Then we went to see the flower fields of Carlsbad. Last, we ended the day at a famous Mexican restaurant in the heart of an old historic town. This was one of the first family trips we did after our new lives as SPV started.

Potato Chip Rock

We got up around 5am and headed to San Diego. The reason why we decided to get up this early was because the hike we chose to do was fully exposed to the sun. Mom wanted to avoid sun exposure at all costs. This hike was a bit hard or mom since there was a good amount of elevation gain over a short distance. While on our hike, we saw firefighters hiking up the same hike we were but with all of their gear on. OMG that looked so difficult. Once we got to the top, we took some great photos atop potato chip rock. I got a gutsy photo mid jump on the rock.

The Flower Fields of Carlsbad

After our hike to Potato Chip Rock, we drove to the Flower Fields of Carlsbad. It was absolutely beautiful and reminded me of a scene right out of a movie. Mom has always wanted to come back and visit this location. While here, we had a mini photo op, filled out a mini stamp book, and enjoyed the fragrant scent of all the flowers around us.

Old Town and Cafe Coyote

The fun filled day of activites left us famished. We decided to go eat at one of mom's favorite resturants, Cafe Coyote, in Old Town San Diego. This place was a bit pricy but super yummy. Mom loves this place because it is rated #1 on yelp with 5,000 reviews and they make their own fresh tortillas. People even line up outside the resturant to buy freshly made tortillas.
