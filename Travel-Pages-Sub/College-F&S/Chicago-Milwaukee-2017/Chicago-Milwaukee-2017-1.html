Chicago/Milwaukee 2017

  • With Joshua, Nathan, Tori, Giovanna, and Terrance
  • Jul 22nd-26th, 2017
  • Northwestern Mutual Conference

Joshua and I decided to attend the Northwestern Mutual Annual Conference in Milwaukee. I was interested in experiencing a large companywide conference and explore more that the Northwestern Mutual role had to offer. The interesting part was that the company did not cover the expense of the flight and we had to pay a conference fee as well. Overall, the cost of the trip was around $1,000. In retrospect, it definitely helped me figure out what I did not want to do after college lol. What I did get out this experience was some friendships and sales soft skills knowledge. Also, exploring the both Chicago and Milwaukee was worth!

Chicago

When booking our flights, Joshua and I found a flight that arrived in Chicago O'Hare International Airport a couple hours before we needed to leave for Milwaukee. Becuase of this, we decided to use that extra time to quickly explore the city of Chicago and then hop on a long distance bus from Chicago to Milwaukee. One of my favorite memories was when we first got to Chicago. We had just gotten off the L train and were going up an escalator. As we started to get closer to street level, we heard the sound of a Jazz band playing. The music got louder and louder until we reached the top of the escalator to a street Jazz band, bright blue sky, and extremely tall skyscrapers. What a way to be introduced to the city of Chicago. Joshua and I went to see the Bean, walk around the city, and eat some deep dish pizza.

Milwaukee

The Northwestern Mutual Annual Conference was an experience. It was the first, big company coporate event I had ever been to. There were happy hours, trainings, hotel parties, and a live Demi Lovato concert!
