Butano Pt.2 2018

  • With Room 5 boys, Dixon, Ungar, and Nate
  • Oct 19th-21st 2018
  • College Boys' Trip

This was our second time going to Butano! This was during college and was such a fun way to reconnect with nature and our college friends. We ate amazing pancakes, hiked throughout the forest, and explored the amazing stream. Although Ungar and I had to leave early on Saturday night, we still made sure that our time at Butano was amazing!

We started the day by eating an amazing breakfast of eggs, bacon, and pancakes. The pancakes were sooooo thick lol. After we had our breakfast, we all gathered outside and headed for the stream and waterfall part of Butano. Some of our friends were having more fun than others haha. There was some amazing hiking, sightseeing, and exploration of wilderness. We did a mini photoshoot near the head of the river.

The river shenanigans were kept to a minimum this time ;). There were some amazing photos of our group. Once we got back, we were able to hangout by the fire, create some amazing drawings, and play some games. Unfortunately, Ungar and I had to leave early to make it back from some college activity stuff on Sunday. We got to look at the beach and eat some delicious ramen on the way back!
