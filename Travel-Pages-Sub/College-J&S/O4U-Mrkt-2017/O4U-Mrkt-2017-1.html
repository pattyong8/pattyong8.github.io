O4U - Marketing 2017

  • With O4U Friends
  • Sept 15th-17th, 2017
  • LGBTQ+ Conference

While applying to a JP Morgan internship position, I learned about the LGBTQ+ professional conference, Out 4 Undergrad, and boy it opened a wide door into a professionally focused community. I applied and was accepted into Out 4 Undergrad Marketing hosted at Pepsico's Headquarters. I got to attend a three day conference full of guest speakers, workshops on marketing, and interviews with sponsor companies. My flight, accommodations, and food was all paid for! This was an opportunity of a lifetime! Very happy to have had this be a milestone in my college life.

This was such an amazing experience. After I got access to my hotel room, I remember feeling nervous about the whole check-in process and excited to the other attendees. I met a ton of amazing people on the first day. The person I connect with the best was Julia Baldaro. She was a bundle of joy and comfort during the entire conference. I remember going to many of the events with her and meeting other attendees through her. After the first day, our sessions continued at the PepsiCo headquarters. There didn’t seem to be a ton of PepsiCo employees around. We had guest speakers talk about their experience as an LGBTQ+ individual in the field of marketing. The food was alright outside of the networking happy hour on the second to last night. There was an open bar and amazing finger food!

Dallas Pride

Picture #1

After the conference was over, we all decided to attend Dallas pride! I think it was a pure coincidence that the conference ended on the day Dallas pride was happening. My flight wasn’t for a bit, so I decided to tag along with the friend group I made. This was my first pride parade and I honestly half expected it not to be that popular since we were in Texas. Boy was I wrong. The street was popping with rainbows! Julia and another friend of ours finessed out way into a random group’s party tent using a bottle of champagne as a bargaining token. We also we in and out of bars in the area that were serving pride themed mixed drinks. So much fun and what a great way to end the conference.
