Napa Valley 2018

  • With College Friends
  • Sept 7th-9th, 2018
  • Ryan's Birthday

Ryan invited our group of Cal friends plus a additional few others to join in a birthday celebration in Napa Valley. We got to do a wine tasting and chill out at an Airbnb provided by his parents. Our housing situation was somewhere called ISIS camp which was only a bit sketchy lol. We also went swimming in a river. Turns out the river was not that sanitary but whatever. Overall this trip was loads of fun and a great opportunity to get away for a weekend.

Russian River Brewery Company

On our way to our Airbnb, we decided to stop by the Russian River Brewery Company for a quick pit stop since it was Ryan’s 21st. OMG the beer was amazing! There was also a fair going on, so we got a chance to hang around and check it out. We got to put a massive snake around our necks! I also raced Matt on the blow-up obstacle course. Once we had our fun we headed to our Airbnb.

We stopped at a mini town near our Airbnb to meet up with rest of the crew. While we were waiting, we entered a random wine tasting room and got a free tasting! We also got a pretty tasty bottle out of it! Turns out our Airbnb was called the “ISIS lodge”. What an interesting name. I called one of the cots on the second story.

Our afternoon adventure started with a swim in the nearby river. After speaking to a random other group near the river, apparently it wasn’t that sanitary….. Ooooooops. We had no other choice but to swim back to our clothes and head back. The rest of the night was full of games, drinks, and good times.

The next day, some of the folks had to leave for Berkeley early and couldn’t stick around for the wine tasting portion of the weekend. Too bad since it was probably the coolest part! Ryan’s dad was able to book a tasting for our group through one of his clients, so we got the full experience. The day started off with a tour of the entire operation. The winery was beautiful. Turns out it was the winery of Francis Ford Coppola, the director of “The Godfather”. We tried so many different wines and go to explore the vineyards. What an experience!
