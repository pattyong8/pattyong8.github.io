I went from studying abroad in China, to visiting Berkeley for a day, to Seattle for my Amazon internship in less than 3 days. When I arrived in Seattle, Olivia was the only person I knew so I immediately reached out to hangout [Picture #1]. Thank goddess for her, else I would have been pretty lonely on day 1 . I am pretty sure I arrived on Sunday afternoon and had to check into my dorm and attend work the very next day.

My office was great and my manager, Alex, seemed chill and a great leader. He spent the first day walking me through the structure of Amazon, our leadership tree, and how Amazon operates. The rest of my team consisted of 3 others: Chris, Ryan, and I think Erich. Chris was the SM, Alex was the Manager, and Ryan & Erich were a lvl 2 FP&A. In the first week, my team was going on a team social with the rest of the department. It ended up being a booze cruise [Pictures #2 & #3]. I got IDed on the boat lol. Also, my office is a dog friendly office [Picture #4]!

So on my first weekend in Seattle, Olivia invited me to Logan’s bday party. I did not know anyone that would be there besides Olivia, Mimi, and Logan. Little did I know that I would meet some of my best friends that night. I remember arriving at Olivia’s apartment complex and waiting at the bottom to be let in. As I was arriving, I saw another group of 4 guys that looked to be my age pop out of a car. I kinda assumed they might also be going to Olivia’s but wasn’t entirely sure. It wasn’t until Mimi came down that I realized we were all attending the same event. It was in that elevator that I met Danny, Eric, Jeff, and Bassel.

We got out of the elevator and made our way to the top floor of Olivia’s apartment. It was quite nice and there was plenty of room for our birthday celebration [Picture #5]. Eric, Danny, and I hit it off and instantly became friends. We sang happy birthday to the crew and then left for a laser light show. Funny side note, I forgot I wasn’t great with hard alcohol and might have accidentally drank more than I should have. The rest of the night was a blur and Eric had to explain that I ran out of the laser show and then disappeared lol. Turns out I was responsible, took an Uber home, and passed out.

In one of the next following days, a few of my other friends, Will, Ryan, Kenneth, etc., went to a restaurant called Taste of Xi’An [Pictures #24 - #26].
