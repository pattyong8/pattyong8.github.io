Woke up around 11am and headed to the train station to go back to Tokyo. Once we got to the train station, we were hungry, so we got some ramen. It was alright and soy sauce based. Once we got on the train, we decided to take a short nap. We were lucky enough to get two seats next to each other. After we woke up, we tried to cross reference our own “owe money” lists. Neither of us were detailed enough in our descriptions, so it was hard to cross reference. I decided to do it later in Taiwan and just sent the list to Noah.

I was also playing the dinosaur card game on my phone. To be honest, it wasn’t fun, but I wanted to pass the time while on the train. After we got to Tokyo, we decided to go to the climbing gym called b-pump [Picture #1]. The fee was quite expensive because we had to pay the one time initiation/registration fee. Total was $30+ per person! However, the gym was worth seeing. The climbs were soooo hard. Like honestly way harder than gyms in the US. I had to stick to the V0-V1 boulders. Those climbs felt like V4-V6 at home haha. We met a guy named Zach from Utah who was in his 2nd month in Tokyo training for the Olympics. Noah spent a ton of time with him and even tried some competition boulders.
