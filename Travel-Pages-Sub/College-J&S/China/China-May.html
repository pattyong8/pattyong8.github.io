Got up at 5:30 to make an early train to Kaohsiung. At the rice in bamboo leaf thing and some fish miso soup. We left for the train. Started with the same old bus and takes us to the MRT. On the MRT, I tried to sleep but couldn’t get myself to haha. It was just super uncomfortable for my neck. I saw a few cute guys on the MRT. We finally got off the MRT after attempting to fall asleep on it. We transitioned to the main Taipei train station. Dad bought his own breakfast and we then went and waited for our train on the waiting chairs right inside of the outdoor station part. Dad noticed a guy from the Asian talk show where they talk about their own experience being married to an Asian spouse. This was the show that dad always watched at home. Very colorful show that I thought was comedy based.
