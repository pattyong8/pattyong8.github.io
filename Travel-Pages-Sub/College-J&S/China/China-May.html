Another fun event thrown by our UCEAP coordinators was a trip to Shanghai Disneyland! We convinced the rest of our travel squad to come as well. They all got tickets and we were on our way to an amazing day at one of the happiest places in the world. We were basically told to figure out transportation on our own but that our tickets would be paid for. Unfortunately, we picked a day where the weather was suboptimal… It was, I kid you not, pouring rain towards the beginning :(. It kinda cleared up as the day went on but never really let up entirely. Ryan and Anotnia decided to bring their sunvisors as anti-rain devices lol [Pictures #127 - #129]. It didn’t work all that well.
