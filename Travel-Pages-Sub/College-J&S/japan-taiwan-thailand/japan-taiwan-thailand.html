Japan/Taiwan/Thailand

  • With Noah, Dad, Davis
  • Jun 19th-Jul 18th, 2019
  • Post college backpacking trip

This trip served as my post graduation period of global exploration. My aim was to experience a multitude of cultures, feast upon exotic foods, and gain a true understanding of backpacking. Full of excitement, I set off to Japan, Taiwan, and Thailand. I traveled with Noah, a good friend I made while interning at Amazon, and Davis, my little brother mentee from high school. We made tons of friends throughout this trip. I am thankful for this life experience. It will be one I will always remember with fond memories and wild moments.
