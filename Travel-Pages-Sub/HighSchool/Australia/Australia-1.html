Australia 2013

  • With CDM Water Polo Team
  • Summer 2013
  • International Water Polo Tournament

This trip was a unique opportunity to be a part of the Varsity squad the summer before my Junior year. I was super excited to go but somewhat nervious that I wouldn't enjoy it since none of my friends were going. I was able to get past this by hanging out with Nigel, Kyle, and getting closer with Jack. My water polo inproved a bunch on this trip. It was very different from the New Zealand trip but just as adventurous.
