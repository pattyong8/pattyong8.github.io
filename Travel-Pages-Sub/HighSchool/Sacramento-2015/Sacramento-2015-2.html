Some Memories From the Junior Board Retreat in Indio

1. This year, more of my friends got onto the board! This included Tatum, Lauren, and Alex. I was so excited to have the same Indo experience but with all these new friends.

2. This was an interesting year since water polo was becoming a bigger deal. I remember that Indio was a bit controversial with my water polo coach. In his mind practice and games came first. I did not blame him. Kyle and I had to figure out how to make both our weekend practice and Y&G retreat work.

Some Memories From Junior Year

1. This was the last year where we got a camp Roberts experience. I wish I had cherished this last opportunity more than I did but no one knew this traditional would end after T&E1. T&E2 ended up taking place in a convention center in Fresno for 1 day. Honestly, it was terrible and everyone complained about the experience. It failed to include one of our favorite parts, being able to bond amongst our chapter after sessions.

2. Lauren and I were co-color group leaders! Whooohooo! We were the purple team and absolutely rocked it.

3. This was the year that I convinced some of my other high school friends to join. These friends previously made fun of me for being a part of this program but as we now know, would eat their words later. It was nice that they joined because it did make the experience more fun. They were JR, Connor, and Garrett.

Some Memories From Senior Year

1. Going into my last year with this program that had been such a large part of my life made me feel both sad and reflective. I had grown so much as a person during these 4 years and truly appreciated what this program had to offer. This year, I made it my goal to give back and mentor others through the program and help create the next generation of fearless, outspoken, and informed NCDMers.

2. I was the Pink group again! Whooohooo! My partner in crime was Julia David! We worked extremely well together and crushed it!

3. The final banquet was an interesting moment full of happiness. I remember the songs, We Are Young and the Story of My Life came on, which JR and I made sure to sing along to lol. The dinner turned into a frenzy of picture taking from NCDM students I had mentored and from my many Y&G friends made over the years.

4. Yes, I cried during the friendship circle. The meeting with the seniors afterwards was so nice and emotional. We continued our friendship circle sharing session and all got super emotional.
