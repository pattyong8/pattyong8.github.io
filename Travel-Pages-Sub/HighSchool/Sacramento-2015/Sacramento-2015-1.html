Some Memories From Freshman Year

1. As a freshman, I was automatically a part of a program called Forum. It was a model of the upperclassmen version of youth and government. It was great for Freshman because everyone was new and all attended the same session together. Our CDM Y&G program was all about taking on leadership roles and being active within all sessions. Because of this, I signed up to be a committee chair and ended up getting the topic of social welfare. It was such a wonderful experience leading a group of my peers through the development of a bill and finding ways to create friendship spaces along the way. I remember bringing candy to our meetings to get folks to open up and feel like our group was special.

2. We also had a Forum advisor, Matt, that came around and helped lead our group in a direction. He was awesome and it was wonderful having him around. Our group all got very close :).

3. Within the CDM Y&G group, I made many friends, some of which are still my best friends today. This program allowed me to establish a friend group that cared about their future and wanted to excel in most things they do. It set me on the right trajectory to success.

Some Memories From the Sophomore Board Trip in Indio

1. I was the only freshman selected to be on the board for CDM Y&G! OMG this was such an honor that many others were fighting over. To be honest, I was very surprised that I was chosen! Wooohooo!

2. So all board members were paired with another board member for our break out color groups. Because I was new, I was paired with our very own Vice President, Emily Petros. She was also in HS ASB which made the pairing that much more familiar and fun! We were the pink group and made sure our group knew we would be the most fun and active group!

3. There were so many ice breakers, team building activities, and time to get to know each other. We played ice breaker games such as over under, Marshmallow stacking, and indian chief. I even got to hangout in the lazy river! This was such a fun opportunity!

Some Memories From Sophomore Year

1. Sophomore year was the year where DoF really took off. Alex, Lauren, Wes, Rafa, and I all rocked it as an NCDM squad. Not only were all of us holding leadership positions, but we were also the most outspoken. Woohoo! NCDM!

2. We got to reunite with some of our Forum friends such as Gabbie and Mackenzie. Y&G provided such an interesting dynamic where high school students from around the state could come together, learn from each other, and become best friends overnight.

3. I got Vincent to join Y&G! He rocked it in Forum and was also a committee chair. This proved to be an amazing opportunity for him as well! Ong Power!
